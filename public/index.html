FAQ

Cars, Running Time & Kilometers

Can I also subscribe to cars with alternative drive systems at Subscribe2Go?

Yes, at Subscribe2Go, we offer a diverse range of vehicles, including those with alternative drive systems. You can choose from electric and hybrid cars, providing you with eco-friendly options that suit your driving preferences and environmental values. Whether you are looking to reduce your carbon footprint or experience the latest in automotive technology, we have options available to meet your needs.

What license plate will my car have?

Your car will come with a standard license plate registered in the country of service. This ensures that your vehicle complies with all local regulations, and you can drive it legally without any additional steps. The license plate will reflect the standard format used in your region, providing a seamless experience as you transition to your new vehicle.

How long are the subscription periods at Subscribe2Go?

Our subscription periods are designed with flexibility in mind. You can start with a subscription as short as 1 month, giving you the freedom to choose how long you want to keep the vehicle. If your needs change, you can easily extend your subscription on a month-to-month basis or opt for a longer-term plan that suits your lifestyle. This flexibility ensures that you only commit to what works best for you.

What happens if I exceed my monthly mileage package?

If you exceed your monthly mileage package, there will be an additional charge based on the number of extra miles driven. We will notify you of the cost per mile over your allotted limit in advance, so there are no surprises. This way, you can manage your driving and adjust your plans accordingly, ensuring you stay within budget or prepare for any additional expenses.

Can I change my car during the term?

Yes, you have the flexibility to change your car during your subscription term. If your needs or preferences shift, simply contact our customer service team to discuss your options. We can assist you in selecting a new vehicle and arrange for a seamless transition, ensuring that you continue to drive a car that perfectly fits your lifestyle.

My subscription is expiring and I would like to keep the car. Can I seamlessly take out a new subscription?

Absolutely, renewing your subscription to keep the same car is a straightforward process. As your current subscription nears its end, you can choose to renew it for another term without any interruption to your service. This allows you to continue driving your preferred vehicle with the same peace of mind and convenience that Subscribe2Go offers.

Is insurance included in the subscription fee?

Yes, comprehensive insurance is included in your monthly subscription fee. This coverage ensures that you are protected against a wide range of risks, including accidents and theft, giving you peace of mind as you drive. There are no additional costs for insurance, and you don't need to worry about arranging it separately—everything is handled for you.

What should I do if my car needs maintenance?

If your car requires maintenance, simply contact our support team, and we will arrange for the necessary service. Maintenance is fully covered under your subscription, meaning you won't have to pay out of pocket for routine repairs or servicing. We'll guide you through the process, from scheduling an appointment to providing a replacement vehicle if needed, so you can stay on the road without any inconvenience.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, there are no hidden fees in our subscription service. The monthly fee you pay covers all costs except for fuel. This includes insurance, maintenance, and any other standard charges associated with your vehicle. We believe in transparency and simplicity, ensuring that you know exactly what you're paying for each month without any unexpected costs.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels: by phone, email, or live chat on our website. Our support team is available to assist you with any questions or issues you might have, ensuring you have a smooth and enjoyable experience with Subscribe2Go. We're here to help, no matter how you prefer to get in touch.
