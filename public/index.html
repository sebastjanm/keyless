FAQ

Cars, running time & kilometers

Can I also subscribe to cars with alternative drive systems at Subcsribe2Go?

Yes, we offer a range of cars with alternative drive systems including electric and hybrid vehicles.

What license plate will my car have?

Your car will have a standard license plate registered in the country of service.

How long are the subscription periods at Subcsribe2Go?

Our subscription periods are flexible, starting from 1 month and can be extended as per your need.

What happens if I exceed my monthly mileage package?

If you exceed your monthly mileage package, an additional fee will be charged based on the extra miles driven.

Can I change my car during the term?

Yes, you can change your car during the subscription term. Contact our customer service for details.

My subscription is expiring and I would like to keep the car. Can I seamlessly take out a new subscription?

Absolutely, you can renew your subscription and continue using the same car without any interruption.

Is insurance included in the subscription fee?

Yes, comprehensive insurance is included in the subscription fee.

What should I do if my car needs maintenance?

Maintenance is covered by us. Simply contact our support team to schedule a service appointment.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, there are no hidden fees. The monthly subscription fee covers all costs except fuel.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support via phone, email, or live chat on our website.
