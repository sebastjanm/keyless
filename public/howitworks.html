What happens if I exceed my mileage limit?

If you exceed your mileage limit, a nominal fee per additional mile will be charged. We'll notify you when you're close to your limit.

Can I add additional drivers to my subscription?

Yes, additional drivers can be added. Each driver must meet our eligibility criteria, and a small fee may apply.

How does the swap process work?

Swapping vehicles is simple. Log into your account, choose your new car, and schedule the exchange. We'll handle the rest.

Is insurance included in the subscription fee?

Yes, comprehensive insurance is included, covering a wide range of risks for your peace of mind.

What should I do if my car needs maintenance?

Contact our support team, and we'll arrange for all necessary maintenance, fully covered under your subscription.
